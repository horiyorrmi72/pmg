Page Title

The Quality Managers Committee(QMC) is a sub-committee of PMG-MAN set up in 1987 originally to discuss and exchange information and ideas on the following:

  1. ISO Certificate
  2. Total Quality Management
  3. Government regulations
  4. Pharmacy regulation
  5. Good manufacturing practice (G.M.P)
  6. Food and Drug Act
  7. Analytical Processing
  8. Manufacturing Standards

With an increase in the number of pharmaceutical firms, the scope has widened over the years.

The quality managers committee also has been involved in the training and development of manpower on the importance of continuous improvement in quality of product and also enhancing quality assurance awareness.

The pharmaceutical manufacturers group of the manufacturers association of Nigeria with acronym PMG-MAN, is the umbrella organization for local drugs manufacturers in Nigeria.

TThe PMG-MAN which was inaugurated in 1983, with twenty founding members and launched by minister of health on October 25th, 1984, now has a membership of over ninety.

Dr. Fidelis Ayebae, the MD/CEO Fidson Healthcare Plc is the Chairman of the PMG-MAN and Mr. Frank Muonemeh is the Executive Secretary of PMG-MAN.

Our Mission

To ensure that safe, quality and affordable medicines are produced in Nigeria.

Our Vision

To achieve 100% National self-sufficiency in the production of high quality and affordable medicines as well as provide employment for Nigerians and to significantly contribute to national development.

PMG-MAN’s work is focused on these key areas:

  • Current Good Manufacturing Practice (cGMP).
  • Social Responsibility – Contributing to government policy formulation and implementation that supports public sector activities.
  • Advocacy and Capacity building.
  • Pharma Exhibition and Pharm Forum on Medicine security and National Self-sufficiency.
  • Self-regulation that delivers added value to members.
  • Providing support for stakeholders and promoting a balanced policy environment – that enables attainment of Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) essentially good health and well-being.
  • Public enlightenment campaigns and public health outreach.
  • Promoting the manufacture of high quality finished medical products and raw materials with Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP).
  • Improving the standard of Pharmaceutical Manufacturing in Nigeria.
  • Creating a forum for interaction and partnership between PMG-MAN and organizations focused on increasing access to healthcare.
  • Promoting and influencing health and medicines policy in various relevant areas, including regulation, trade and other strategic areas.
  • Developing and promoting the Pharmaceutical Manufacturing Sector towards contributing to economy as well as other areas of National Development.
